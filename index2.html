Values I Work By

  • Clarity over complexity — simple systems scale.
  • Build with users, not for them — real feedback early and often.
  • Craft and reliability — details matter, reliability earns trust.

Why I Build

I’m driven by the challenge of turning messy, human problems into dependable systems people enjoy using. My background in mechanical engineering taught me to reason from first principles; my time in customer success sharpened how I listen, frame problems, and prioritize what actually improves outcomes. I care about products that help people grow — especially at the moment they’re deciding what to do next.
